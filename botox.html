Botox

Neurotoxin injectables work by temporarily relaxing specific facial muscles to smooth lines and prevent further deepening of wrinkles. We offer a range of options—Botox®, Xeomin®, Dysport®, and Nuceiva®—to cater to your unique skin needs, helping you achieve a refreshed, youthful appearance. These treatments are ideal for targeting crow’s feet, frown lines, forehead lines, and other areas prone to expression lines.

Areas to Treat:

  • Forehead lines
  • Crows feet
  • Frown lines
  • Brow lift
  • Neck bands
  • Masseter (facial slimming)
  • Bunny lines
  • Gummy smile
  • Lip flip
  • Vertical lip lines
  • Dimply chin
  • Excessive underarm sweating
